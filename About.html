About Us

At Richie’s Delight Catering, we bring the authentic flavors of Nigeria to your table. From Jollof Rice, Egusi Soup, Suya, Akara, and Moi-Moi, every dish is prepared with love, taste, and tradition.

We specialize in catering for weddings, birthdays, corporate events, and everyday cravings. Our mission is simple: “Delight every customer with taste and tradition.”

Our Story

Richie’s Delight Catering was founded with a passion to share authentic Nigerian cuisine. From small family gatherings to grand events, we’ve been serving love on plates for over 10 years. Every meal carries our promise of taste, tradition, and excellence.
